dignity-first

  • dignity-first is my endeavour to support the vulnerable urban domestic workers & daily wage earners in this hour of crisis. A large number of them have lost their jobs due to the current situation. With their savings running out,their families are going hungry!
  • My mission is to make sure that THEY DO NOT SLEEP HUNGRY.
  • The aim is to do this by providing them an OPPORTUNITY TO EARN and feed their families with dignity.
  • How it started - I got the idea after witnessing the scarcity of masks among the vulnerable poor and at the same time I saw the plight of the domestic helpers (maids) who had lost jobs around me.My idea provides livelihood to the maids & their families and also helps slow down spread of the virus by distributing free masks among the urban poor.
  • I now plan to reach out and support the families of at least 20 domestic workers / daily wage earners.
  • DIGNITY OF LABOUR coupled with SAFETY!
